Information

This page explains what the system is, what data is collected, and how reports are handled.

Confidentiality

Reports are stored securely and only accessible to authorised staff (admin).

Anonymous option

If submitted anonymously, the system does not collect a name or email. The admin can still view the report content and evidence (if provided).

Tracking your report

After submitting a report, you will receive a unique reference ID. This can be used to track the progress of your report through the tracking page.

What happens next?

The wellbeing/admin team reviews the report and updates its status. Users can track updates using their reference ID. If contact details are provided, users will receive an email update notification when there has been any changes or updates on the report.

How are reports handled

Reports are managed through a secure admin dashboard where authorised staff can view reports and update report, and respond appropriately.
